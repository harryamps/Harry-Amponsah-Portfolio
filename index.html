With over 6 years of professional experience, I've partnered with more than 450 clients - from early-stage startups building their first product to established enterprises modernising their digital infrastructure. Across every engagement, my focus has been the same: close the gap between how something looks and how well it actually works.

My core expertise spans React, JavaScript (ES6+), TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Node.js, Figma, and Firebase - but beyond the stack, what I bring to a team is the ability to think in systems. I care about component architecture, performance budgets, accessibility, and writing code that the next developer will actually thank you for.

I hold a BSc in Computer Science from the University of Cape Coast (UCC), and I stay sharp through continuous learning - because in this field, last year's best practice is this year's technical debt.

Beyond client work, I build tools that solve real problems - like TaskFlow, an internal notification system I engineered from scratch to keep distributed teams aligned across Email, SMS, and WhatsApp, without the noise of manual follow-ups.

I'm deliberate about the work I take on. Not because I'm selective for the sake of it - but because I'd rather do fewer things exceptionally than many things adequately.

When I'm not shipping code, I'm thinking about product, mentoring developers, or figuring out how to raise the bar for what gets built out of Ghana.
